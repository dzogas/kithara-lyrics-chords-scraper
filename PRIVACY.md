# Privacy Policy

**Kithara Lyrics & Chords Scraper** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.  

The extension only accesses the currently active [kithara.to](https://kithara.to) song page when the user clicks the **Scrape Lyrics & Chords** button, in order to read lyrics and chords and convert them into ChordPro format.  

No information is shared with third parties, and no browsing history or user data is retained.
